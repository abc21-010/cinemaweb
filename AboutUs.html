About Cinemato

Our history

Cinemato is a movies theater company started in 2016 by Brilliant Bokamoso Hill in Gaborone, Botswana. we have a huge movies library, and we get to show movies that are only in cinemas, and we provide all of this at affordable prices. We are the very first Cinema to collaborate with leading technological brand like Apple, Microsoft, Google and Sony
